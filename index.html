Mars Planet Overview

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System. Often referred to as the Red Planet due to iron oxide on its surface, Mars is a primary target for NASA Mars exploration. It features a thin atmosphere and incredible landmarks like Olympus Mons and Valles Marineris.
